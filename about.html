David

David Anthony’s eye for irreverent beauty was evident from his boyhood in Missouri. His degree in Architecture with an emphasis in Interior Design from Southwest Missouri State University was a mere continuation of what was already inevitable. And he has since laid claim to his rightful place as D.C.’s most elevated, elegant, and aspirational designer of both public and private spaces.

Lan

Dubbed David Anthony’s PR Guru, Lan is the voice behind the David Anthony Chenault brand. Raised among the arts and antiquities of Asia, Lan later graduated from Boston College with a degree in Art History, solidifying her as a force to be reckoned with at the intersection of business and design.

Her propensity for art and style placed her alongside giants like Neiman Marcus and Martha Stewart Living before joining the studio of David Anthony Chenault in 2003.

Lan’s shared passion for David’s vision shines through their more than 20 year partnership, and counting.
